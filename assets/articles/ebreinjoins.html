Ebre-in!

On October 21st, 2024, Ebrein has officially been accepted into MAGIC. The Ebreinian Government had sent its application a day prior, following (according to President Rhodes) "numerous recommendations by a close ally [of Ebrein]."

The news have been welcomed with enthusiasm by the Ebreinian population, who feels more secure both from internal and external threats.
