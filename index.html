What is MAGIC?

MAGIC is a defensive military alliance between the nations of Cozio, Dushina, Ebrein, the ZU, Rakcen, Pathonia, Karafuto, and the Ascendant Hive. The primary goal of the MAGIC international organisation is for the protection of each member state from foreign and domestic threats.

>MAGIC on the world stage.

MAGIC History

MAGIC was founded March 16th, 2024 by Tsar Fluff of Dushina to increase international cooperation beween the nations of Dushina, Maxifaer and Pathonia. Later, several nations would be added to the MAGIC organisation, becoming one of the largest defensive alliances in the world.

Become a MAGE

To become a MAGE (member of MAGIC), simply telegram Mittens R. Fluff through any government channel and list the reasons as to why you wish to become a member of the MAGIC. If accepted, you will become the latest member of the MAGIC family.
